About Me

My name is Alexander James Begg and I was born on October 30, 1985. I currently live in an apartment in Los Angeles, California (Mar Vista, West L.A.), and I am majoring in Media Arts & Animation at The Art Institute of California - Los Angeles, in Santa Monica, California. I grew up in La Puente, an eastern suburb of Los Angeles, and I started doing animation on my own since before high school and took an animation course my last two years of high school. I am currently working with a friend at a visual effects place in Hollywood, California doing motion graphics for TV shows, award shows, etc.

I am a producer of two films, I am an Executive Producer of a student film made in high school called Locker 767: The First Semester, finished at the end of 2003, that was made on almost no budget and sold many DVDs and won awards at some film festivals. The other I donated money to and got a Producer title, it is to raise money for the Global Women Fund, it is an IMAX film that will premiere in Hollywood in 2006 called The 1 Second Film.

Outside of the industry I do a lot of volunteer community work and I am an Eagle Scout and Assistant Scoutmaster in the Boy Scouts of America. I help staff and organize many camps and events, and I am an active member of the Order of the Arrow, Scouting's national honor society.
