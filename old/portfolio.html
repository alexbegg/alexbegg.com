Portfolio

I am a Media Arts & Animation major in college, and am currently aspiring to become a music video director. I know local artists and DJs in Los Angeles area, have worked on graphics for MTV programs, and have been studying and enjoying many music videos since I was a kid. My goal is always to create a visually fulfilling video that work with the beats and the melody of the music.

Some of my favorite Music Video Directors are Samuel Bayer, who has some of the most intense music videos (music videos from Green Day's American Idiot album as well as Blink-182's "Stay Together For The Kids", and his first being Nirvana's "Smell Like Teen Spirit"), Joseph Kahn who has excellent cinematography and editing in his videos (Blink 182's "Always", Moby's "We Are All Made Of Stars", and Eminem's "Without Me" among a long list of others), and Spike Jonze (some of the Weezer and Beastie Boys videos and skateboard videos, as well as being producer and co-creator of Jackass).
